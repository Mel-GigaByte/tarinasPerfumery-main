WELCOME

Welcome to Tarina's Perfumery, where every scent tells a story and true artistry and aroma meet.

As the heart and soul behind this aromatic journey, the multi-award-winning Tarina personally crafts all signature Parfums and Fragrances, each one bearing its own enchanting story.

With a dedication to offering the finest fragrances at affordable rates, Tarina invites you to explore her bespoke creations as well as our 'Inspired By' collection, which pays homage to well-known scents on the market.

Every bottle holds not just a scent but a narrative waiting to be discovered.

About Us

The Essence of Tarina's Perfumery

Founded by Tarina Els, an extraordinary example of the power and tenacity that can be achieved through hard work. As a cancer survivor, Tarina turned her battle into inspiration by channeling her love for fragrances into a thriving business in 2022.

Her remarkable story includes significant achievements, such as winning the South Africa Council for Business Women Rising Star award, and the Business Chambers' Entrepreneur of the Year award.

These accolades reflect not only her business acumen but also her ability to turn adversity into a source of strength and motivation. It is the spirit of tenacity and perfection that serves as the foundation upon which Tarina's Perfumery is constructed, which is what makes it a venture that is both unique and inspiring.... FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What type of perfume should I ask for at Tarina's Perfumery
We recommend asking for 'Parfum', which offers a more concentrated fragrance compared to 'Eau de Parfum'
What is used to mix the fragrances at Tarina's Perfumery?
Our fragrances are mixed with the highest grade of Perfumers Ethanol, ensuring a premium quality scent experience.
How long do the mixtures at Tarina's Perfumery mature before bottling?
All our mixtures mature for a minimum of 7 days. For optimal quality, we prefer a maturation period of 1 to 12 months before bottling.
Can I create a custom scent at Tarina's Perfumery?
Yes, you can! We offer a unique custom perfume service where you can create a fragrance that is exclusively yours.
Do you offer a satisfaction guarantee with your fragrances?
Absolutely. We have an unconditional money-back guarantee, so you can explore our fragrances with complete confidence.
How can I become a Tarina's Perfumery Agent?
We're always looking for passionate individuals to join our team. Contact us for more information on how to become a part of the Tarina's Perfumery family.
What makes Tarina's Perfumery fragrances unique?
Our fragrances are crafted from the finest ingredients, including exclusive essences from Grasse, France, and are known for their luxurious quality and unique blends.
