Tarina Els

Located in the charming town of Mossel Bay, Tarina's Perfumery bears witness to the unwavering determination of its namesake founder, Tarina. Enthusiasm, tenacity, and the surmounting of personal health obstacles—including a successful fight against cancer—are the threads that bind her story together.

Tarina's journey is a testament to the power of following one's heart.

After Tarina's interest in fragrances blossomed into a full-fledged business, what had begun as a hobby quickly became her life's work. Her love for crafting quality perfumes that stand the test of time, initially intended as gifts for close friends and family, evolved into the flourishing business she runs today.

Tarina's Perfumery was formally established on May 19, 2022. Since its launch as a registered business, Tarina's Perfumery has experienced immense growth. Consequently, her original small collection of fifteen scents has expanded to her current portfolio of one hundred and ten, featuring eight of her very own signature perfumes. With this assortment, she has showcased her skill and imagination in fragrance creation, catering to a wide range of tastes.

‘’You don’t have to pay a fortune to smell like a million dollars.’’

Tarina

Tarina strongly advocates for the idea that luxury shouldn't come with an exorbitant price tag, believing it can broaden accessibility to high-quality products and experiences. True to her motto, she offers superb quality perfumes at surprisingly affordable prices. With a diverse selection of budget-friendly scents, including her own captivating creations, Tarina ensures that luxury is within reach for all. This ethos strikes a chord with individuals who admire sophistication and value practicality and affordability.
